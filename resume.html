Who We Are

Akbar and Associates is a leading structural consulting engineering firm, established in 1976 by Mr. Akbar Gul. We specialize in the planning and design of diverse construction projects across Pakistan and internationally. With nearly five decades of expertise, our contributions span industrial facilities, high-rise buildings, hospitals, historical monuments and bridges.
